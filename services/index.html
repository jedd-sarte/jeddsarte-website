Common questions

Things people ask before we work together.

Do you work with businesses outside the U.S.?

My current focus is exclusively on U.S.-based clients. My vertical experience, platform knowledge, and competitive benchmarks are all built around the U.S. market.

Why no prices on the services page?

Every engagement is scoped to your actual situation - the number of platforms, campaigns, your ad spend, and what you need built. A flat price would either overcharge simple projects or undercut complex ones. Let's talk scope first.

How long before I see results?

Honest answer: it depends on your baseline. New accounts with strong tracking typically see meaningful data within 30–60 days. Optimized accounts running clean infrastructure see improvements in weeks. I'll give you a realistic timeline during our discovery call.

Do I need a minimum ad spend?

I include minimums on the individual service descriptions above. Generally, below $1,500/mo in ad spend, the management overhead isn't worth it for either of us. At that level, I'd point you toward self-serve resources instead.

Can you work with our existing agency or in-house team?

Yes. I've collaborated with in-house marketing teams and other agencies on specific pieces - audit, tracking setup, landing pages - without taking over the full account. Scope it during the call.

What does the onboarding process look like?

Discovery call → proposal with scope and timeline → kickoff → audit phase → strategy → build → launch → optimize. Most clients are live within 2–3 weeks of signing, depending on complexity.
